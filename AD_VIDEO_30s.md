# CloudBill — 30 Second Ad Script

## Concept: "Bill Podra? (போடுறா?)"
One catchy question that sticks: "Bill podra?" (Did you bill it?)

---

## SCRIPT

### [0-4s]
**Girl at a tea shop counter, owner gives her a paper bill**
Girl looks at the messy handwritten bill, makes a face.
**Girl:** "Anna... 2026 la ippadiya?" (In 2026, still like this?)

### [4-9s]
**She takes his phone, taps 3 times on CloudBill**
Sound effect: tap, tap, tap
**Girl:** "Name type pannu. Price auto varum. GST auto varum. Done."

### [9-14s]
**Phone screen shows beautiful invoice with UPI QR**
She shows it to the shop owner. He's surprised.
**Girl:** "WhatsApp la send pannu. Customer QR scan panni pay pannuva."

### [14-18s]
**Cut to: Different shop — cloth store owner billing on CloudBill**
**Cut to: Freelancer on laptop creating invoice**
**Cut to: Small factory owner checking dashboard**
**Girl (voiceover):** "Yaaruku vendaalum. Entha business-kum."
(For anyone. Any business.)

### [18-24s]
**Girl back on screen, holds phone showing green Balance number**
**Girl:** "Evlo vanthuchu, evlo selavu, evlo balance — oru paarvai."
(How much came in, spent, balance — one look.)

### [24-28s]
**Girl looks at camera, raises eyebrow, smirks**
**Girl:** "Innum paper bill-a?"
(Still paper bills?)

### [28-30s]
**Logo pops in with a satisfying sound**
**Text:** CloudBill
**Below:** Bill podra? Download free.

---

## WHY THIS WORKS

| Element | Why it sticks |
|---|---|
| "Bill podra?" | Everyday Tamil phrase. People will repeat it. |
| "2026 la ippadiya?" | Shames old methods with humor, not aggression |
| Tap-tap-tap | 3 taps = simple. Visual + sound = memorable |
| Real shop settings | Tea shop, cloth store, factory — audience sees themselves |
| "Innum paper bill-a?" | Ends with a challenge. Makes them think. |
| No feature dump | Only shows: auto-fill, WhatsApp, QR, balance |

## CATCHY LINES (pick for social media reels)

- "Bill podra?" — main hook
- "2026 la ippadiya?" — shame with humor
- "Name type pannu, baaki ellam auto" — the promise
- "Innum paper bill-a?" — closer
- "Oru paarvai la un business theriyum" — one look shows your business

## MUSIC
- First 4 seconds: quiet, real ambient shop sounds
- After girl taps phone: upbeat Tamil-style beat drops
- Last 6 seconds: beat + logo sound
